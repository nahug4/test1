About Nalam Diabetic Prevention: A New Beginning for Health

Nalam Diabetic Prevention is a social initiative dedicated to tackling the rising tide of diabetes in India. We believe that knowledge is power, and early detection saves lives. By partnering with local communities and medical professionals, we provide free, accurate HbA1c testing and comprehensive awareness campaigns. Our goal is to empower individuals to take control of their health journey, leading to a healthier, more vibrant India.

We leverage technology to simplify the process, from registration to result delivery, ensuring privacy and accessibility for all.

Ready to Get Tested? It's Simple & Free!

Understanding your HbA1c level is the first step towards a healthier future. Our free, accurate tests give you a 3-month average of your blood glucose.

  • ✅ **Easy Registration:** Sign up online or at a camp.
  • ✅ **Professional Testing:** Conducted by certified phlebotomists.
  • ✅ **Secure Results:** Delivered confidentially via WhatsApp.
  • ✅ **Expert Guidance:** Referrals to government health centers for follow-up.
